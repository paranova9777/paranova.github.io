  • Fire and Water

This piece is based on a scene from the movie Logan. I created it during my time in digital foundations class where we were practicing the use of the pen tool in illustrator. It focuses on her ferce emotions so using a red color background best conveyed that rage and dark atmosphere.



This is an word dichotomy based off the word fire and water. The objective of this assignment was to give the feeling of the two words by purely using color themselves. The fire pattern on the left has a sharp shapes and the water on right has soft edges.
